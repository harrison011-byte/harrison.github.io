Active Conservation Projects

We're working on the ground to protect endangered species and restore habitats

Amazon Rainforest Protection

Brazil, South America

Protecting 1 million hectares of Amazon rainforest from deforestation and illegal logging through community-based conservation.

$750,000 raised Goal: $1M

Rhino Anti-Poaching Initiative

Kenya, Africa

Deploying ranger teams and technology to protect endangered black and white rhinos from poaching in national parks.

$300,000 raised Goal: $500K

Coral Reef Restoration

Great Barrier Reef, Australia

Restoring damaged coral reefs through innovative coral farming and transplantation techniques to rebuild marine ecosystems.
